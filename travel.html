Travel Square

There are many options to get into New Delhi International airport but note that you will most likely arrive later at night if you are coming from North America, Africa or Europe. On the way to New Delhi, you will lose a day (i.e. if you depart December 27 evening from the US, you will land in New Delhi the night of December 28. Keeping that in mind, I suggest departing on December 26, so that you make it into town by the night of December 27. For flights out of New Delhi, they will most likely be between the hours of 11 PM-4 AM. Keep that in mind as you finalize dates. I also recommend looking at two one-way tickets as they may be cheaper than a roundtrip.

Visa Information

For citizens of most Western countries, eVisa is okay. Apply 4-30 days prior (should take 2-3 days). I recommend applying 20 days in advance. Please check your nation’s embassy website. If you have any questions, please let me know.
